The Document Store is a digital system designed to organize, manage, and enhance your legal documents. It's created to simplify your work processes, even if you're not particularly tech-savvy. Think of it as an intelligent filing system that not only stores your documents but also helps you work with them more effectively.

## Setting Up Your Document Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Vault">
    Navigate to the Document Store tab and select "Create New Vault." You can then upload your documents by dragging and dropping files into the designated area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link to Workflows">
    Connect your document vault to your work processes so that Lawme can access relevant information when needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Access Permissions">
    Define who can view and edit specific documents to maintain security and confidentiality.
  </Step>
</Steps>
