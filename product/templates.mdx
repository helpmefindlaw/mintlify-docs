Leverage the power of automated templating to streamline routine legal documents, allowing your team to work faster and more accurately. Our platform enables you to not only create new templates but also use existing ones within your organisation, making it easier to standardise documents across your firm.

## Getting Started with Templates

To begin using templates in Lawme:

1. Navigate to the Templates section in your dashboard
2. Choose from existing templates or create a new one
3. Customize the template to fit your specific needs
4. Save and use the template for future document creation

For more detailed instructions on creating and using templates, check out our [Template Creation Guide](/guides/template-creation).
