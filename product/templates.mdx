## Getting Started with Smart Templates

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Template Hub">
    Go to the Templates section in your Lawme dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick or Make a Template">
    Choose from our curated library or use our template creator to build a custom one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customise with Smart Help">
    Fine-tune your template with suggested improvements and firm-specific tweaks.

  </Step>
